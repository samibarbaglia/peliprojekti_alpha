GUIDE

Hey! This page will guide you to play our game, Flight Simulator 2: The Flight Simulatorning!

First of all, you will need to choose a name for yourself pilot! Basically anything will do, just keep it under X amount, okay?

After you’ve chosen yourself a worthy nickname (For example: “Captain Weinermeister”!), you get to choose what kind of plane will you be piloting! You can either choose a big commercial jet or a smaller private plane. The commercial jet can go farther in one flight than the smaller plane, but it also releases more CO2 to the atmosphere, and vice versa!

Next up, our super awesome high-tech giga-mega-super-duper-computer HAL-10000 will randomly choose a destination for you! After HAL-10000 has done its thing, your mission is to get to the destination airport with as minimal CO2-emissions as possible!

To play the game, you only have to choose your next destination airport from the map! The game will end when you reach your destination (duh!) !
